Target 9.1
Target 9.1
Target 9.1 states: Develop sustainable, resilient and inclusive infrastructures. UN definition: Develop quality, reliable, sustainable and resilient infrastructure, including regional and transborder infrastructure, to support economic development and human well-being, with a focus on affordable and equitable access for all.
Why?
Target 9.1 is important because having access to proper infrastructure is important. Access to the internet can allow people to learn about new and useful things. Access to proper sewage systems can stop the spread of dangerous waterborne diseases. Or having proper roads can help people transport things much quicker and easier. Having cell towers and mobile phones can allow people to easily communicate with each other. Many of these things can seem small. But when they are added up they can have a significant impact on someone's life.
