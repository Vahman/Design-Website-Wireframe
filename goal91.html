What is Goal 9.1?

The target of goal 9.1 is to develop infrastructure which is resilient,sustainable and inclusive.

In Goal 9.1 there are 2 indicators. These are Goal 9.1.1 which is focused on getting road access to people in rural areas.

Goal 9.1.2 is focused on air and rail travel and allowing anyone around the world to use them.
