Goal 9 is one of the SDG goals. Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation. Taken from the Goal 9 page on the UN website. Why is this important? Goal 9 is important because it aims to help everyone have access to the infrastructure they need. This can include basic needs such as roads or sewage systems. But it also includes having internet and phone access. Many of these we take for granted as we have them, and can always use them. But for many people around the world that isn't true. For example in Chad on 0.8% of their roads are paved. This makes it very difficult for people to be able to travel from one place to another. Because of this it can be hard to get certain goods that someone need. It can even cause it to be hard for someone to get to their school. Poor infrastructure might seem like it only affects somethings which may not be necessary but it is important as it allows people to get other useful things because of it.
