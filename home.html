What is Goal 9?

Goal 9: Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation

Why is Goal 9 important?

Many people around the world do not have access to proper infrastructure.

Because of this there are many things that they cannot do which you might take for granted.

Without access to some key things such as roads, people can't improve their situation in life.

Using the above example of roads, if you didn't have roads you cannot transport goods to other places.

This would stop you from being able to make money since you can't sell your product.

There are many other important bits of infrastructure that many people don't have.

By acheiveing this goal we can pave the road to other goals we need to acheive.
